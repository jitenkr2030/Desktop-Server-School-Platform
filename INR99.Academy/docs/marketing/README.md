## Quick Reference

### Key Messages

**Tagline:** "UPI for education - Quality learning at ₹99/month"

**Value Propositions:**
1. Zero investment for schools
2. 10x more affordable than competitors
3. Comprehensive content (500+ courses, 10,000+ lessons)
4. Works on any mobile device
5. Offline capability

**Pricing:**
- Per Student: ₹99/month
- Class Package: ₹999/month (40 students)
- School License: ₹4,999-9,999/month

## Target Audience

### Primary
- Affordable private schools (₹500-2,000/month tuition)
- Low-cost international schools
- Budget residential schools

### Secondary
- Government schools (PPP model)
- School chains and franchises
- NGO-run schools

## Sales Process

### 1. Outreach
- Cold calling/WhatsApp
- Email campaigns
- LinkedIn connection

### 2. Demo
- 5-minute product demo
- Show key features
- Answer questions

### 3. Pilot
- 30-day free trial
- Teacher training
- Monitor metrics

### 4. Close
- Present pilot results
- Discuss pricing options
- Sign partnership agreement
